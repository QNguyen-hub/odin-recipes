Pho Recipes

Here you will learn how to make a traditional Vietnamese dish call Pho. It is a hot noodle soup you could eat any time of the year. This is a quick way to make pho when in a rush. The traditional takes 8 hours plus and requires a lot more prep work.

  1. Ingredients
    • Steak
    • Thin Rice Noodels
    • Pho Broth
    • Spices
      • Star Anise
      • Whole Cloves
      • Cinnamon Sticks
      • Cardamom Pods
      • Coriander Seeds
    • Fish Sauce
  2. How To Make the Pho Soup
    • Prep the Protein
      • Slice the beef as thinly as possible
    • Make the Broth
      • Put the spices, charred onion and ginger into a pot and shimmer for 30 minutes
      • Strain out and discard all of the onions, ginger and spices
    • Prep the Noodles
      • Cook the noodles in a pot of boiling water
      • Drain the noodles in a strainer
    • Assemble
      • Add a handful of noodles to the bowl
      • Topped with the thinly slice beef
      • Pour the pho broth on top of the beef for it to cook
      • Top with garnishes to your liking
    • ENJOY!!!!
