Banh Mi

Here I will show you how to make Banh Mi, which is a vietnamese sandwhich full of flavor. I will not going over on how to make it from scratch but how to assemble one.

  1. Ingredients
    • Vietnamese cold cuts
    • French baguettes
    • Pate
    • Mayo
    • Pickled carrot
    • cucumber, coriander/cilantor,green onion, chilli
    • Maggi seasoning
  2. Assembling
    1. Split the French baguettes in half from top to bottom, don't cut it all the way in half
    2. Smear the pate then the mayo on one side of the baguettes
    3. Lay the ham, cucumber stripes, carrot, green onion, coriander/cilantro
    4. Add a bit of chilli if you like it to have a kick, then add Maggi seasoning just a slight dash of it not to much.
  3. ENJOY!!!!
